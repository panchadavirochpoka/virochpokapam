Artist Statement

My work explores the intersection of digital fabrication, organic forms, and emotional cartography. Through large format prints, 3D modeling, and video art, I create immersive narratives that question material permanence. Each project becomes a dialogue between texture, light, and memory — inviting the viewer into a soft yet bold visual universe. Pink isn't just a color; it's a state of vulnerability and power.
